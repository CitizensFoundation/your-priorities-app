What you can do

From ideas to decisions, together

Your Priorities empowers groups of any size to speak with one voice and organize around ideas — from a single neighbourhood to a whole nation.

lightbulb

Idea generation

Citizens propose their own solutions, organized into projects, communities and categories — with images, video, audio and map locations.

balance

Constructive debate

Instead of comment threads, people add independent points for and against each idea. This unique format keeps debate civil and makes trolling nearly impossible.

how_to_vote

Voting and prioritization

Flexible voting with hearts, arrows or custom emoji, and up to four evaluation criteria — so the best ideas rise to the top.

checklist

Surveys and polls

From quick polls to advanced surveys with skip logic — gather structured input alongside open idea generation.

savings

Participatory budgeting

Let residents decide how public money is spent. Used in hundreds of participatory budgeting processes together with Open Active Voting.

verified_user

Secure and inclusive

Government electronic IDs, email or anonymous participation, fraud detection and full GDPR compliance — engagement everyone can trust.

Artificial intelligence, built in

AI that amplifies people — never replaces them

Your Priorities pairs collective intelligence with state-of-the-art AI to make participation easier, safer and more insightful, in any language.

Automated translation and speech-to-text in nearly 200 languages, so everyone can participate in their own language — side by side.

Every contribution is automatically screened for toxicity and moderators are alerted instantly — keeping conversations safe and welcoming around the clock.

psychology

Policy Synth agents

Our open source Policy Synth framework orchestrates teams of AI agents that research, draft and evolve policy solutions — with human votes always in the loop, as used by the New Jersey AI Task Force.

insights

AI analytics

Automatic clustering of ideas, topic analysis and rich dashboards turn thousands of contributions into clear, actionable insight.

Wiki surveys

All Our Ideas, now part of Your Priorities

All Our Ideas is the much-loved open source wiki survey tool, created at Princeton University. Participants are shown two ideas at a time and simply pick the one they prefer — every vote sharpens a crowd-ranked list of what matters most, and anyone can add new ideas of their own.

The Citizens Foundation has taken over its open source maintenance, and All Our Ideas is now available as a group type right inside Your Priorities — so you can run pairwise wiki surveys alongside idea generation, debates and participatory budgeting.
